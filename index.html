Welcome! I am a climate scientist working in the Climate & Global Dynamics Laboratory at the National Center for Atmospheric Research. My research interests center around the impacts of climate change on land-atmosphere interactions and climate variability. I am also interested in machine learning approaches to climate science and modeling, including quantifying uncertainty in model projections of climate change.
