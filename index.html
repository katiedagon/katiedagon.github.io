Welcome! I am a climate scientist working in the Climate & Global Dynamics Laboratory at the National Center for Atmospheric Research. My research interests include modeling the impacts of climate change on land-atmosphere interactions and the terrestrial hydrologic cycle. I am also interested in machine learning approaches to quantifying uncertainty in climate models.
